Mobile Note Taker

Using mobile phone to take notes could be hard for now, an app that could get screen and voice input to generate notes will help people without ipad. Editing ppt, sharing notes could be added to this app to make it works better to help students.

Solution Collector

It could be common to have bug during programming, fixes will cost time and help people in the next time while meet it, however, it could be hard to remember all those solutions by brain. This app will record the solutions, environment and libs, provide clear details for review, and allows the user to find solutions from other people.
